Raff Rafting Co

Raff Rafting Co. isn’t just another rafting company—it’s an adrenaline-fueled experience designed for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. Operating along the fiercest rivers in the Pacific Northwest, Raff Rafting has built a reputation for pushing the limits of adventure.

HISTORY

Raff Rafting Co. has been a legend among thrill-seekers for over two decades. Founded by Pius Godwin, the company started with just two rafts and a dream—to bring the ultimate white-water experience to anyone daring enough to ride the rapids. Today, Raff Rafting Co. operates on some of the most exhilarating stretches of the Arkansas and Colorado Rivers, offering everything from beginner-friendly floats to heart-pounding Class V expeditions. Each trip is guided by seasoned experts who know every twist and turn of the waters, ensuring both safety and excitement.
